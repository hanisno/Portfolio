Kopikaw

Coffee shop style guide

Kopikaw is a coffee shop concept serving specialty coffee and tea blends with fun and exciting flavors. I designed a brand identity and style guide to support their personality and messaging.

Download full style guide

Objective

To develop an entire brand identity and a set of design deliverables to launch Kopikaw and support their brand personality and messaging.

Problem

Kopikaw has funding and a great location in a popular and artsy suburb but needs a brand that fits their location, character and unique range of products. They want their brand to stand out and become the go-to place to get coffee that will energize and excite their customers.

Solution

Build a complete brand identity style guide which includes a cohesive color scheme, logo, type scale, icons, and imagery to capture the energetic, playful, and bold personality of the brand. This will create a strong brand identity that the target audience will love, inspiring trust and loyalty that boosts business.

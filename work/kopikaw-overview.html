Kopikaw Coffee

Objective

To develop an entire brand identity and a set of design deliverables to launch Kopikaw Coffee shop and support their brand personality and messaging.

Problem

Kopikaw Coffee has funding and a great location in a popular and artsy suburb and now needs a brand that fits their location and unique range of products. They are serving specialty coffee and tea blends as well as a small variety of snacks.

Solution

Build a complete brand identity style guide which includes a cohesive color scheme, logo, typescale, icons, and imagery to capture the energetic, playful, and bold personality of the brand. This will create a strong brand identity that the target audience will love, inspiring trust and loyalty that boosts business.

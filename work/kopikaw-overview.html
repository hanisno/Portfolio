Kopikaw

Coffee shop branding & landing page

Kopikaw is a coffee shop concept serving specialty coffee and tea blends with fun and exciting flavors. I designed a brand identity and landing page to help this new local coffee shop launch its grand opening.

Objective

To develop an entire brand identity and a set of design deliverables to help Kopikaw coffee shop launch their grand opening and support their brand personality and messaging.

Problem

Kopikaw has funding and a great location in a popular and artsy suburb but needs a brand that fits their location, character, and unique range of products, as well as a way to deliver information about their brand and grand opening to their customers. They want their brand to stand out among the locals and become the go-to place to get coffee.

Solution

Build a complete brand identity package which includes a cohesive color scheme, logo, type scale, icons, and imagery, and integrating this in a landing page for Kopikaw that allows customers to easily find out about the shopâ€™s grand opening and sign up to their newsletter. Energetic, playful, and bold are the key personality traits for this brand.

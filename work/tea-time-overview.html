Description

Tea Time is a JavaScript-based interactive clock I developed that suggests a type of tea based on the time of the day. Users can select custom tea times or click on the "Thirs-tea?" button for a special drink suggestion. Its basic functionality is inspired by the LOLCat Clock but personalized to my liking of tea. It is mobile and tablet friendly.

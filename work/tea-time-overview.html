Tea Time

Description

Tea Time is a JavaScript-based interactive clock that suggests a type of tea based on the time of the day. Users can input custom tea times or click on the "Thirs-tea?" button for a special drink suggestion. Its basic functionality is inspired from the LOLCat Clock by Skillcrush but personalized to my like of tea.

Sit Conmigo

Chair preorder website design

Sit Conmigo is an ethical, sustainable chair company concept. I designed a website for them so that their customers can view new chair designs and preorder them.

Download full style guide

Area

Web design

Deliverables

Brand identity, style guide

Tech

Figma

Objective

To design a website that encourages customers to preorder their chairs and introduce them to the Sit Conmigo brand. The design should be a one-page website, with links to the Pre-Order, About and Contact section, mobile-ready, and have a large call-to-action header.

Problem

Sit Conmigo has designed four chairs so far and feels it’s the right time to launch their website, but first needs a website design made. They need a page that showcases their chairs and entice customers to preorder, highlight their mission as a fair trade and fair labor designer, and visually communicate the personality of their company.

Solution

Develop the mobile, tablet, and desktop design comp along with color and type styles that captures the trustworthy, stylish, and sincere personality and mission of the company. This will create a strong impression on Sit Conmigo’s customers that inspires trust and entice them to preorder their chairs.
